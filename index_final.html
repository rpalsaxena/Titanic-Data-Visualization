Survival of Passengers according to Ticket Fare

In this graph, we categorized passengers in 3 categories:

  • VIP : 10% passengers paying maximum fare
  • Lower Class : Passengers travelling with fare=$0, i.e, they maybe staff or staff's friend travelling with free passes
  • General : Normal passenge who are paying more than 0$ and less than that pad by VIPs.

This graph clearly illustrates that VIPs survived more. VIPs survival chance is 97%(female) & 36%(male).

Survival rate of Lower class people is very less. Only 7%(male)& 0%(female) survived.

Survival rate of General class people is more than Lower but less than VIPs. Only 69%(female) & 18%(male) survived.

Survival of Passengers according to Gender(Male / Female)

Using the following visualization, we are trying to understand the survival rate of passengers according to Gender. During our analysis phase, we analyzed that 577 males and 314 females were travelling. It means there were more Male passengers in graph.

Graph shows that survival rate of Female passengers is 74% and Male passengers is 19%.

Its clear that females were preferred over male passengers.

Survival according to Socio-economic status Upper Class (1st), Middle Class(2nd), Lower Class(3rd)

In analysis phase, we analyzed that:

  • No. of Class 1 people : 216 , 24.24% of total
  • No. of Class 2 people : 184 , 20.65% of total

From the above visualizations of the dataset of passengers on Titanic Ship, we can guess that partiality was done in saving passengers.
